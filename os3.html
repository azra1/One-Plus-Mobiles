OxygenOS
Breathe it all in.



We created OxygenOS with a simple goal: to perfect the balance between power and simplicity.
 We heard you like your OS clean, comfortable, and easy to use right out of the box. So do we. 
        If you prefer to tinker, OxygenOS also includes an array of robust power features. 
                  Customization is still an important aspect of the OxygenOS game.






Introducing Marshmallow
The next big step
forward
Marshmallow didn’t just make things sweeter, it also
 made things a lot more power efficient. We’ve 
optimized the Doze feature specifically for the 
OnePlus 3, so your battery lasts even longer. A robust
 App Permissions feature protects your data and 
privacy. And with ‘Now on Tap’ you can find 
information relevant to whatever is on your screen
 with just the press of a button.
