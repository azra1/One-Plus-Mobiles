Customize and 
Personalize
-------



Use Dark Mode for a sleek look that is easy on
 the eyes. Change your accent color to express
 your personal style. OxygenOS also supports
 custom icons packs in almost any format,
 allowing you to drastically alter the look
 and feel of your device. Combined with
 our StyleSwap covers, your OnePlus 2
 can be personalized in a near-infinite
 number of combinations.









          Take Back Control
                    ------


    Protecting your data should be simple and easy.
    Our App Permissions feature allows access to
   information - like location, identity, or contacts -
    to be blocked on a per-app basis. Is a particular
  app sending you too many notifications? Turn
    them off. It’s your phone; don’t let your apps 
                      control it.









  
Enhanced Audio
   -----


    We've integrated Waves MaxxAudio® sound
 design technologies into OxygenOS for the best
 audio performance possible on a mobile device.
 Our intuitive settings let you toggle between audio
 presets right from your volume panel. Enhanced
 for speakers, headphones, or other connected 
devices, MaxxAudio® brings amazing life to
 your music, movies, and games.


















  
A Smarter Keyboard
-----


SwiftKey Keyboard uses Artificial Intelligence to automatically learn the words that matter to you and how you
 like to type. That means autocorrect and predictive text that actually works, helping you get more done without
 fussing over typos. Supporting almost 100 languages, it’s as global as the OnePlus 2 itself.
