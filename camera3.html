Camera





































More advanced for better imaging
Thanks to a superior 16MP sensor, pictures and videos taken on the OnePlus 3 exude incredible detail and clarity. Our exclusive Smart 
Capture technology makes taking the perfect photo easier and more worry-free than ever before. Electronic (EIS) and optical (OIS) 
stabilization technologies work in harmony to ensure that pictures and videos are crisp and smooth, while a robust manual model and
 RAW image support are sure to satisfy even the most discerning photography enthusiast.

Smart Capture
The perfect photos made easy
Our exclusive technologies effortlessly capture the ideal shot, no matter the environment. In difficult lighting conditions, High-Dynamic
 Range (HDR) automatically produces well-balanced photos. Grainy pictures are a thing of the past with Dynamic De-noise, which takes 
several photos and compares them to reduce noise and improve clarity. Advanced point-and-click shooting has never been easier.

Manual Control
Professional precision
If you’d rather be in control of each and every aspect of a shot’s composition, we’ve got you covered. Our robust manual mode provides all 
of the benefits of a DSLR with none of the weight. Once active, you can personally fine-tune exposure, ISO, focus, and more. The OnePlus 
3 also supports RAW image files out of the box for expert post-shot editing.



Dual Stabilization
Two technologies, one amazing result
Two stabilization technologies ensure that blurriness won’t stand in the way of your perfect picture or video.
 Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) mechanically compensates for jolts and shakes to prevent blurry photos. Paired
 with Electronic Image Stabilization (EIS), which digitally cancels out unwanted movements, photos and videos 
always come out just the way you planned.
